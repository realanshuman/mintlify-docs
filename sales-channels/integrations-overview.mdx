Tokensupply splits multi-channel selling into two complementary sections: **Integrations**, where you connect external platforms and supply credentials, and **Sales Channel**, where you manage the active listings that result from those connections. Together they give you a single workspace for every marketplace and storefront you sell through — without jumping between tabs or dashboards.

## Two sections, one workflow

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Integrations" icon="plug">
    Connect external platforms — Shopify, G2A, Eneba, Bamboo Card, and others. Each connector has its own page for entering API credentials, reviewing connection status, and configuring sync settings.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sales Channel" icon="store">
    Manage active listings across every connected channel in one place. Filter by channel, toggle Active / Auto Deliver on individual mappings, and review product–offer relationships without opening each integration separately.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Both **Integrations** and **Sales Channel** are restricted to **Super Admin** and **Admin** roles. Users with the Vendor or Support role do not see either section in the sidebar.
</Note>

## Getting started with a new channel

When your store has no integrations configured yet, the Integrations and Sales Channel pages display placeholder cards for each supported platform. These placeholders guide you to set up your first connection — click any card to open that platform's setup page and enter your credentials.

Once you connect at least one channel, the Sales Channel view populates with your product mappings and you can begin managing listings in bulk.
