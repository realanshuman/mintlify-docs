## Purchase Orders

Purchase Orders (POs) are how you procure stock from vendors. Open them from **Purchases → Purchase Orders** (`/purchase-orders`).

### List view

The list has filter tabs across the top — **All / Draft / Confirmed / Payment Pending / Paid / Closed** — plus search (PO number, vendor, product), vendor filter, sort (PO number, date, total, vendor) and CSV export. Pagination is 15 per page.

### Create a PO

Click **New Purchase Order**. The form is grouped into cards:

- **Vendor** — pick from active vendors. The badge shows Individual or Business. Selecting a vendor pre-fills currency and payment terms from the vendor profile.
- **Product** — pick a product. If a vendor↔product mapping exists in **Vendor Offers**, the unit price and delivery time are auto-filled.
- **Quantity, Unit Price, Currency** — override the auto-filled price if needed; currency defaults to the vendor's currency.
- **Tax** — for Business vendors the tax rate and label are derived from your store country vs the vendor country (e.g. **GST 18%** for an Indian vendor selling to an Indian store, **0%** intra-EU reverse charge, etc.). Toggle **Tax Inclusive** if the unit price already includes tax. Individual vendors are forced to 0% with no override.
- **Cost Adjustments** — add discounts or additional charges (shipping, handling, FX fees) as labelled line items.
- **Terms & Delivery** — payment terms, due date, delivery method (Email by default), vendor note, tags (max 8).
- **Invoice (optional)** — attach a vendor invoice file at creation; it lands in **Pending Review**.

Use **Save as Draft** to keep editing, or **Confirm** to send the PO. Confirming triggers an email to the vendor and your admins via `send-po-email`.

### PO numbering

PO numbers are generated by the `generate_po_number` RPC and follow **\`PO-{VENDOR_CODE}-{SEQUENCE}\`** — for example `PO-SLR-001-0042`. Sequences are scoped per vendor, per store.

### Lifecycle (5 statuses)

| Status (DB) | Shown as | What it means |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Draft** | Draft | Saved, not yet sent to the vendor |
| **Sent** | **Confirmed** | Shared with the vendor, awaiting payment |
| **Payment Pending** | Payment Pending | Payment in progress |
| **Paid** | Paid | Payment confirmed |
| **Closed** | Closed | Procurement complete and locked |

\> Stock collection is **not** a separate status — it runs in parallel with the lifecycle and is tracked on the same page.

Move forward with the **Mark as \[Next Status\]** button (chevron right). To revert one step use the **Undo** action. Each transition records its own timestamp (`sent_at`, `payment_pending_at`, `paid_at`, `closed_at`) and triggers a status-change email.

### Invoice workflow

- Vendor (or admin) uploads an invoice file → status becomes **Pending Review**
- Admin opens the **Review** dialog and **Approves** or **Rejects** with optional notes
- The decision and notes are stored on the PO and surfaced in the timeline

A PO must be at **Sent** or later before an invoice can be uploaded.

### Stock workflow

Once the PO is **Sent**, the vendor (or admin) can submit stock from the **Stock** card:

- Paste keys one per line — for **Account** products use `username | password` per line
- Optional bulk file import (CSV / TXT)
- Optional **expiry date** applied to all items
- Real-time duplicate detection against existing inventory; duplicates are flagged inline with **⚠️ DUPLICATE**
- Submissions are capped at the remaining quantity

When the submitted count reaches the PO quantity, `stock_collected_at` is recorded and the items appear under the linked product's **Inventory** tab. **Reset Stock** clears all linked inventory and resets the marker.

### More Actions

The "…" menu offers **Edit**, **Duplicate** (pre-fills a new PO from this one), **Download PDF** and **Delete** (typed DELETE confirmation). Deletion unlinks any tickets and removes PO notes.

### PO PDF

Generated client-side, the PDF includes both parties' addresses, tax IDs, line items, tax breakdown, totals, and the vendor's bank details (IFSC / SWIFT / IBAN / UPI / PayPal etc.). Individual-vendor POs print a Tax Declaration block.

### Notes & collaboration

The **PO Notes** card on the detail page is a team-only thread (vendors don't see it). Each note shows the author's name and a colour-coded role badge (Admin / Support). Use it for internal comments separate from the vendor-facing **Vendor Note** field.

### Reminders

`po-due-date-cron` runs daily and sends reminder emails / in-app notifications when a PO is approaching or past its due date.
