## Welcome to TokenSupply

TokenSupply is the operating system for digital-goods sellers — manage products, inventory keys, vendors, sales channels, customers and delivery from a single workspace.

### Step 1: Sign up and create your store

Sign up at **tokensupply.in**. After verifying your email you are taken to **Create Store** where you set the store name, country and default currency. The account that creates the store becomes its **Super Admin** automatically.

### Step 2: Configure business details

Open **Settings → General** and fill in:

- **Store Name** — your public brand
- **Company Name** and **Legal Name** — used on invoices and POs
- **Support Email**, **Address**, **GST/PAN** and **Default Currency**
- **Logo** — appears in the topbar, customer portal, emails and PDFs

Branding (logo + colours) and the **Order ID Format** are configured on the same **General** tab.

### Step 3: Customise your storefront

Open **Settings → Store Page** to edit your storefront slug (`token_id`) and toggle whether the public store page is visible. Your store URL becomes `tokensupply.in/s/{token_id}` (or your custom domain once verified).

### Step 4: Bring your team

Use **Team → Invite Member** to add Admins, Support staff or Vendors. Each role unlocks a tailored sidebar and permissions.

### Roles at a glance

| Role | Best for |
| --- | --- |
| **Super Admin** | Store owner, full access including billing |
| **Admin** | Operations leads — team, billing, integrations |
| **Support** | Day-to-day operations, tickets, inventory |
| **Vendor** | External suppliers — limited to their own POs and offers |
