## Internal Team Chat

The **Messages** module (sidebar → **Messages**) gives your team Slack-style channels and direct messages, plus the ability to **link an entity** (order, ticket, vendor, PO, customer) directly into a thread.

### Highlights

- Color-coded role badges (Admin / Support / Vendor)
- Entity links open the related detail page in one click
- Mobile collapses to a single-pane experience for chat-on-the-go
- Vendors see a constrained version scoped to threads they participate in
