§ 01

## Overview

This Refund Policy applies to all purchases of TokenSupply subscriptions, credits, and paid features made through the Platform operated by GENFLIX COMMERCE PRIVATE LIMITED.

All payments for the Service are processed by Paddle.com Inc. (paddle.com) acting as our Merchant of Record ("Paddle"). Because Paddle is the seller of record for your purchase, Paddle handles all refund processing on our behalf in accordance with this policy and Paddle's Buyer Terms.

§ 02

## Refund Window

You may request a full refund within thirty (30) days of your original purchase or renewal date for any subscription, credit pack, or one-time paid feature, for any reason.

Pro-rated refunds for unused portions of annual subscriptions may be considered on a case-by-case basis outside the 30-day window.

NoteThe 30-day window applies to first-time purchases and each subsequent renewal independently. Refund eligibility is not affected by partial use of the Service during the window.

§ 03

## How to Request a Refund

All refund requests are handled by Paddle as Merchant of Record. To submit a request:

- Visit https://www.paddle.net and sign in with the email address used for the purchase, or
- Submit a request through Paddle's help centre at https://help.paddle.com, or
- Use the refund form at https://paddle.net/checkout/refund.

You may also email us at billing@tokensupply.in and we will assist by forwarding your request to Paddle. Please include your order ID or the email address used at checkout to help us locate the transaction.

§ 04

## Processing & Timing

Approved refunds are issued by Paddle to the original payment method. Most refunds are processed within 3–5 business days of approval, though the time for the credit to appear on your statement depends on your bank or card issuer (typically 5–10 additional business days).

Where local law in your jurisdiction provides longer or stronger refund rights (for example, statutory consumer rights in the EU/UK), those rights apply in addition to this policy.

§ 05

## Credits & Already-Delivered Keys

Credits already consumed to deliver digital keys to your Customers represent fulfilled value and may be deducted from any refund amount where the underlying delivery cannot be reversed.

If a delivery is reversed (refunded to your Customer, flagged as fraud, or failed due to a verifiable platform error), the consumed Credits may be restored to your account at our discretion.

Unused Credits remaining in your account at the time of a refund request will be cancelled as part of the refund.

§ 06

## Exclusions

The following are generally not eligible for refund, except where required by applicable law or Paddle's Buyer Terms:

- Charges older than 30 days from the date of payment.
- Accounts terminated by us due to confirmed fraud, abuse, or material breach of our Terms of Service.
- Third-party fees, taxes, or charges that have already been remitted to tax authorities and cannot be recovered.

Even where an exclusion applies, you may still escalate your request to Paddle, whose decision as Merchant of Record is final.

§ 07

## Chargebacks

Please contact us or Paddle before initiating a chargeback with your bank or card issuer. Most billing concerns can be resolved more quickly through a refund request than through the chargeback process.

Unjustified chargebacks may result in account suspension pending resolution.

§ 08

## Contact

Refunds: submit via Paddle at https://www.paddle.net or https://help.paddle.com.

Billing questions: billing@tokensupply.in. General support: support@tokensupply.in.
