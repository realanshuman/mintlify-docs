## Integrations & Sales Channels

TokenSupply separates two related concepts in the sidebar:

### Integrations

`Integrations` (sidebar → **Integrations**) is where you **connect** external platforms — Shopify, G2A, distributors like Bamboo Card and so on. Each connector has its own detail page for credentials, status and sync settings.

### Sales Channel

`Sales Channel` (sidebar → **Sales Channel**) is where you **manage active listings** across all your connected channels in one place. Filter by channel, toggle Active / Auto Deliver, and review mappings without jumping into each integration.

### Onboarding

New stores start with a clean slate — placeholder marketplace cards guide you to connect a channel before listings appear anywhere.

### Availability

Both pages are restricted to **Super Admin** and **Admin** roles. Vendors and Support do not see Integrations or Sales Channel in the sidebar.
