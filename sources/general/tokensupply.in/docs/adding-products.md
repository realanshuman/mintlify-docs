## Adding & Managing Products

### Creating a product

- Go to **Products → Add Product**
- Use the centred, step-by-step layout to fill in:
- **Product Name**, **SKU**, **Brand**
- **Functional Category** — pick from the strict catalog (Game, Software, Gift Card, Subscription, etc.)
- **Product Type** — Key, Account or Gift Card
- **Listing Configuration** — delivery time (Instant / Within 1 hour / Within 24 hours)

### Product statuses

- **Enabled** — live and sellable
- **Disabled** — temporarily hidden
- **Discontinued** — archived

### Detail page

The product detail page uses the standard layout: icon-only breadcrumb, profile header, quick stats strip and underline tabs for **Overview**, **Inventory**, **Sales Channels**, **Vendor Offers** and **Activity**.

### List view

`Products` uses a high-density card list with **Lowest Price** highlighting so you can spot pricing outliers across vendor offers at a glance.
