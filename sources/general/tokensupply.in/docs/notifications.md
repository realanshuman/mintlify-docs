## Notifications

### Types

- **PO updates** — status changes, invoice and stock approvals, due-date reminders
- **Ticket events** — new tickets, replies, resolutions
- **Team** — joins (vendor vs staff), role changes
- **Expiring inventory** — keys expiring within your configured window
- **Low stock** — products under your configured threshold
- **Stock file rejected** — vendor needs to re-upload
- **Low credits** — billing approaching limit

### Where

Click the **bell icon** in the topbar for the in-app notification timeline. Configure email preferences per notification type — including digest frequency (Instant / Hourly / Daily / Weekly) — in **Settings → Notifications**.

### Delivery

Email notifications use your SMTP (with Resend as fallback). Critical events also surface in-app even if email is paused.
