## Tickets, Replacements & Refunds

### Creating a ticket

Go to **Tickets → Create Ticket**. Set:

- **Title**, **Description**, **Issue Type** (Quality, Delivery, Payment, Invalid Keys, Expired Keys, …)
- **Severity** — Low / Medium / High / Critical
- Link to the related Product, Vendor, Order or PO

### Lifecycle (5 stages)

**Open → In Progress → Awaiting Vendor / Customer → Resolved → Closed**, with replacement badges shown on the timeline.

### SLA tracking

Each ticket has an SLA deadline visualised by a colour-coded progress bar (green / amber / red).

### Resolution types

- **Replacement** — issue a new key (links to the in-place replacement workflow)
- **Refund** — refund the order; portal links auto-revoke and keys may return to inventory

### Linked keys

Search inventory by key value or reference and link items to the ticket. Every link/unlink and reveal is logged in the timeline.

### Internal notes & attachments

Add internal notes (with author attribution and role badges) and attach screenshots or documents — useful for vendor disputes and audit trails.

### AI-assisted summary

Long ticket threads can be summarised with the built-in AI summarisation (Lovable AI gateway) so handovers between team members are quick and accurate.
