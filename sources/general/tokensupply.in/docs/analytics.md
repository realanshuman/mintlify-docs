## Analytics

The **Analytics** page (sidebar → **Analytics**) is a SaaS-style dashboard built on Recharts. The classic **Reports** entry sits next to it and is currently a Coming Soon placeholder for scheduled exports.

### Tabs

- **Overview** — revenue, orders, AOV and channel mix over the selected range
- **Products** — top sellers, lowest-margin items, drill-down per product
- **Vendors** — vendor performance, on-time delivery, quality (ticket-derived)
- **Customers** — Individual vs Business mix, repeat-rate

### Revenue calculation

Revenue is **net of refunds** and aggregated by **completed orders only** — pending and failed orders are excluded.

### Date range

Use the date picker for Last 7 days, Last 30 days, Last 6 months or a custom range. KPI cards and charts both update.

### Vendor Analytics

Vendors see their own dashboard at **Analytics** in their portal (route `/vendor-analytics`) with 6-month trend lines and KPIs — useful for monthly business reviews.
