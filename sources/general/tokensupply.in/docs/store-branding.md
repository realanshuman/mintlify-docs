## Store Branding

### Uploading a logo

Go to **Settings → General → Branding** and upload a PNG or SVG. The image is stored in the public `store-logos` bucket and appears in:

- The topbar of the admin app
- The customer delivery portal
- Email headers
- Invoice and PO PDFs

### Colours

The brand palette uses TokenSupply's amber accent on a deep navy header (`#121621`) with an off-white workspace background (`#f7f8f9`) — designed for long working sessions and clear data legibility.
