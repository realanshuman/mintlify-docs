## Billing, Credits & Plans

### Credit model

TokenSupply uses a simple usage model: **1 key delivered = 1 credit consumed**. Refunds do not refund credits.

### Plans

| Plan | Best for | Highlights |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Free** | Trying TokenSupply | Limited credits, core features |
| **Pro** | Growing stores | Generous credits, unlimited integrations, priority support |

### Where

Open **Billing & Plans** from the sidebar (Super Admin and Admin only). The page shows a live usage widget with credits consumed, credits remaining and a transaction summary. Currency formatting is mapped dynamically per store.

### Upgrading

Upgrade from the dashboard upgrade banner or **Billing & Plans → Upgrade**. Changes take effect immediately.
