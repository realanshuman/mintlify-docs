## Frequently asked.

How do credits work?+

1 credit = 1 key delivered to a buyer. Manual deliveries, marketplace orders, replacements — all consume one credit per key. Unused credits roll over while your subscription is active.

What happens when I run out of credits?+

Auto-delivery pauses until you top up or upgrade. Your data, integrations, and inventory stay intact.

Can I switch or cancel anytime?+

Yes. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel from your billing page. No lock-ins, no cancellation fees.

Do you charge per integration?+

No. Connect every supported marketplace and channel on any plan.
