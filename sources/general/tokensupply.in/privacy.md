§ 01

## Introduction

GENFLIX COMMERCE PRIVATE LIMITED, operating the TokenSupply platform ("we", "us", "our"), respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data we process about you. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the rights you have over it.

This Policy applies to the TokenSupply Platform, our website at tokensupply.in, and all related services. By using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy.

§ 02

## Data Controller

For data submitted by Store Owners and their team members, TokenSupply acts as the data controller for account, billing, and platform-usage data, and as a data processor for Customer and Vendor data uploaded into a Store.

Store Owners are independently responsible as data controllers for the personal data of their Customers, Vendors, and team members that they upload to the Service.

§ 03

## Information We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to operate the Service. Categories include:

Account

Name, email, password hash, profile photo, role, and 2FA TOTP seed (encrypted).

Store Data

Store name, legal name, address, contact details, branding assets, and order ID prefix.

Operational

Vendors, customers, products, inventory keys (masked at rest), purchase orders, invoices, tickets, messages.

Billing

Plan, credits, invoice history. Card details are processed by our payment processor and never stored on our servers.

Usage

Pages visited, features used, IP address, browser type, device, timestamps, and error logs for diagnostics.

Communications

Emails, in-app messages, and ticket correspondence between you and us, or between users within a Store.

NoteWe do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us data, contact privacy@tokensupply.in for deletion.

§ 04

## How We Use Your Information

We process personal data for the following purposes:

- To provide, maintain, and operate the Service — including authentication, multi-tenant isolation, and feature delivery.
- To process payments, manage credits, and issue invoices.
- To send transactional communications (delivery confirmations, password resets, vendor invites, billing receipts).
- To provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
- To monitor performance, debug issues, and improve product quality and security.
- To detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
- To comply with legal obligations, court orders, and regulatory requirements.

§ 05

## Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable law (such as the EU GDPR or India's DPDP Act) requires a legal basis, we rely on:

- Contract — to provide the Service you have signed up for.
- Legitimate interests — to operate, secure, and improve the Service in ways you would reasonably expect.
- Consent — for optional features such as marketing emails, where required.
- Legal obligation — for tax, audit, and law-enforcement compliance.

§ 06

## How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only as described below:

Subprocessors

Trusted infrastructure providers (cloud hosting, email delivery, error monitoring, AI summarization) bound by data-processing agreements.

Payments (Merchant of Record)

Payments are processed by Paddle.com Inc. (paddle.com), our Merchant of Record, who receives billing, transaction, tax, and limited customer data (name, email, billing address, card metadata) to complete purchases, manage subscriptions, issue invoices, handle refunds, and comply with tax law.

Within a Store

Data inside a Store is visible to authorized members of that Store based on their role.

Integrations

When you enable G2A, Shopify, Bamboo Card, or similar, relevant data is exchanged per your configuration.

Legal

When required by law, regulation, court order, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

Business Transfer

If GENFLIX COMMERCE PRIVATE LIMITED is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale, your data may be transferred under equivalent protections.

§ 07

## Subprocessors

We use the following categories of subprocessors to deliver the Service:

- Cloud hosting and managed database (Lovable Cloud / Supabase) — primary infrastructure and storage.
- Payments and Merchant of Record (Paddle.com Inc., paddle.com) — payment processing, subscription billing, tax calculation and remittance, invoicing, and refund handling. Paddle is the seller of record for all purchases made through the Service.
- Email delivery (your configured SMTP provider, with Resend as fallback) — transactional and notification emails.
- AI gateway (Lovable AI) — optional ticket summarization and assistive features.
- Marketplace APIs (G2A, Shopify, Bamboo Card) — only when you explicitly enable them.

A current list of subprocessors is available on request from privacy@tokensupply.in.

§ 08

## Data Security

We use industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal data, including:

- Encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest for sensitive fields, including inventory keys.
- Row-level security policies enforcing strict per-Store isolation at the database layer.
- Two-factor authentication (TOTP) for high-privilege accounts.
- Short-lived signed URLs for private file access (e.g. vendor agreements).
- Continuous logging, anomaly detection, and access-control reviews.

ImportantNo system is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping credentials confidential and enabling 2FA where available.

§ 09

## Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to provide the Service and to comply with legal, accounting, and reporting obligations.

When a Store is deleted, operational data is purged or anonymized within 30 days, except where retention is required for tax, audit, or fraud-prevention purposes (typically up to 7 years).

Vendor records that have linked transactions are unlinked rather than hard-deleted to preserve historical accuracy.

§ 10

## Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- Rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
- Erasure — request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.
- Restriction or objection — limit how we process your data.
- Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
- Withdraw consent — at any time where processing is based on consent.
- Lodge a complaint — with your local data protection authority.

To exercise these rights, email privacy@tokensupply.in. We will respond within 30 days. Most account data can also be managed directly from your account settings.

§ 11

## Cookies & Tracking

We use a minimal set of essential cookies to keep you signed in and remember preferences. We do not use third-party advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

We may use first-party analytics to understand product usage in aggregate, anonymized form. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling essential cookies will impair your ability to use the Service.

§ 12

## International Transfers

Personal data may be processed in countries other than your own where our infrastructure providers operate. Where data is transferred outside your jurisdiction, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms.

§ 13

## Automated Decision-Making

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you. AI-assisted features (e.g. ticket summarization) are advisory only and reviewed by humans.

§ 14

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the Service, the law, or our practices. When we make material changes, we will update the "Effective" date and notify you via email or in-app notification at least seven (7) days before the changes take effect.

§ 15

## Contact Us

For privacy questions, data subject requests, or to report a concern, contact our privacy team at privacy@tokensupply.in. For security incidents, contact security@tokensupply.in.

If you are dissatisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
