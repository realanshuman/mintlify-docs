Your TokenSupply account settings cover two areas: your personal profile (name, avatar, email) and security controls (two-factor authentication, sessions, and password recovery). Access them from the user menu in the topbar or by navigating directly to `/account`. Keeping your account secure protects both your store data and the customers and vendors who depend on it.

## Personal profile settings

Navigate to **user menu → /account** (or go to `/account` directly) to open your account page. It has two tabs: **Profile** and **Security**.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Profile tab" icon="user">
    Update your display name, upload or change your avatar, and change the email address associated with your account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Security tab" icon="shield-halved">
    Enable or manage two-factor authentication (TOTP), view active sessions, and access password recovery options.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Two-factor authentication (2FA)

TokenSupply supports **TOTP (Time-based One-Time Password)** for two-factor authentication. Any standard authenticator app works — including 1Password, Authy, and Google Authenticator.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Security tab">
    Go to `/account` and click the **Security** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable 2FA">
    Click **Enable Two-Factor Authentication**. A QR code and setup key are displayed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan with your authenticator app">
    Open your authenticator app and scan the QR code. The issuer registers as **tokensupply.in**, so the code appears under a clearly labelled entry in your app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the confirmation code">
    Type the current 6-digit TOTP code from your authenticator app to confirm the setup was successful.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your backup codes">
    Store the provided backup codes in a safe place. You will need them if you lose access to your authenticator app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once enabled, you'll be prompted for your authenticator code at every sign-in — meaning stolen credentials alone are not enough to access your account.

<Warning>
  If you lose access to your authenticator app and your backup codes, account recovery requires contacting TokenSupply support. Store your backup codes somewhere secure (a password manager works well).
</Warning>

## Sessions and password recovery

- Signing out from any active session terminates access immediately — there is no delay or grace period.
- To reset your password, navigate to **/reset-password**. The recovery flow is designed to avoid permanent lockouts — even if you are mid-2FA setup when requesting a reset.

<Tip>
  If you are locked out and cannot receive a password reset email (for example, if your from-email SMTP is misconfigured), ensure Resend fallback is active — critical account emails route through it automatically.
</Tip>

## Vendor portal sign-in

The vendor portal is entirely separate from the main TokenSupply platform and has its own distinct sign-in page.

| Aspect | Staff accounts | Vendor accounts |
|--------|---------------|-----------------|
| **Sign-in URL** | Main platform | `/s/{token_id}/vendor-portal` |
| **Self sign-up** | Invite only | Invite only |
| **Multi-store access** | One store per account | Same email can be a vendor on multiple stores |
| **Conflict with store ownership** | N/A | A vendor email can also own a separate TokenSupply store |

When a vendor with an existing TokenSupply account is invited to a new store and clicks their setup link, they are redirected to the sign-in page with their email pre-filled — they are not prompted to create a new password, since their account already exists.

<Note>
  Each vendor invite is scoped to that specific store. Accepting an invite on one store does not grant access to any other store, even if the same email is invited to multiple stores.
</Note>

## Account deletion

The **Delete Account** option is only available to **Super Admins** of store-owning accounts. Team members (Admin, Support) and Vendors do not see this option — it is hidden from their account pages entirely.

<Warning>
  Deleting your account is permanent and irreversible. All store data — products, orders, inventory, and team memberships — is removed. Ensure you have exported any records you need before proceeding.
</Warning>
