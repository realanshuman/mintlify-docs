Your store's visual identity flows from a single place: **Settings → General → Branding**. Upload your logo once and TokenSupply automatically applies it across every customer-facing surface, from the admin topbar to emailed invoices. Keeping your branding consistent here means you never have to update individual templates or documents when your logo changes.

## Uploading your logo

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open General Settings">
    In the sidebar, click **Settings**, then select the **General** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate the Branding section">
    Scroll to the **Branding** section on the General tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your logo file">
    Click the upload area and select a **PNG** or **SVG** file. SVG is recommended for sharp rendering at all sizes. Your image is stored securely in the public `store-logos` bucket.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click **Save** to apply the new logo. The update propagates immediately across all surfaces listed below.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Where your logo appears

Once uploaded, your logo is automatically displayed in the following places:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Admin Topbar" icon="display">
    Appears in the top-left corner of the admin app so your team always sees your brand while working.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Customer Delivery Portal" icon="box-open">
    Shown to customers when they access their order delivery page, reinforcing trust at the moment of fulfillment.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email Headers" icon="envelope-open">
    Displayed at the top of every transactional email sent through your SMTP or via the Resend fallback.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Invoice & PO PDFs" icon="file-pdf">
    Embedded in generated invoice and purchase order PDFs so your documents look professional and branded.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Colour settings

TokenSupply uses a carefully considered default palette optimised for long working sessions and data legibility:

| Element | Colour |
|---------|--------|
| **Header background** | Deep navy — `#121621` |
| **Workspace background** | Off-white — `#f7f8f9` |
| **Accent** | TokenSupply amber |

<Tip>
  The deep navy header paired with an off-white workspace background is intentionally designed to reduce eye strain during extended use. If you customise colours, test your palette at length before committing.
</Tip>

## Supported file formats

| Format | Recommended use |
|--------|----------------|
| **SVG** | Best choice — scales perfectly at any resolution, smallest file size |
| **PNG** | Use a high-resolution version (at least 400 × 400 px) for clear rendering on retina displays |

<Note>
  Avoid JPEG or GIF logos — lossy compression and limited transparency support can cause your logo to appear blurry or with a white background box in email clients.
</Note>
