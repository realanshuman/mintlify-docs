The **Settings** module gives you centralised control over your store's identity, communication, domain, and visibility. Access it by clicking **Settings** in the sidebar — it opens in its own layout with five horizontal tabs, each scoping a distinct area of configuration. Two additional top-level items — **Templates** and **Billing & Plans** — live separately in the sidebar and are not part of the Settings tab group.

## Settings tabs

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="General" icon="sliders">
    Manage your business details (name, address, contact email), upload your logo, set brand colours, configure your Order ID Format, and control store-wide preferences.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email Setup" icon="envelope">
    Connect your own SMTP server so all transactional emails — deliveries, notifications, vendor invites — send from your domain with your from-name and from-email.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Domain" icon="globe">
    Point a custom domain at your TokenSupply storefront. Add DNS records, trigger verification, and let the platform auto-provision an SSL certificate once DNS resolves.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Notifications" icon="bell">
    Choose which events trigger an email to you and your team, and set the digest frequency (Instant, Hourly, Daily, or Weekly) per event type.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Store Page" icon="store">
    Set your public storefront slug and toggle your store's visibility between public and private without affecting existing orders or deliveries.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Separate top-level items

<Note>
  **Templates** and **Billing & Plans** are standalone sidebar entries — not tabs inside Settings. Navigate to them directly from the sidebar when you need to manage email/document templates or your subscription and credit usage.
</Note>

## Quick-reference table

| Tab | What you configure |
|-----|--------------------|
| **General** | Business details · branding (logo + colours) · Order ID Format · store address and contact |
| **Email Setup** | SMTP host · port · credentials · from-email · from-name · encryption · test send |
| **Domain** | Custom domain · DNS records · SSL provisioning |
| **Notifications** | Per-event email preferences · digest frequency |
| **Store Page** | Public storefront slug · store visibility |
