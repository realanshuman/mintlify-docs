Tokensupply uses a straightforward credit model: you pay for delivery, not for seats or features. Every time a key is delivered to a buyer — automatically through an integration or manually via a logged order — one credit is consumed. This page explains how credits work, what each plan includes, and how to manage your billing.

## How the credit model works

**1 key delivered = 1 credit consumed.** Credits are spent at the moment of delivery, regardless of which channel or method fulfilled the order.

<Info>
  Refunds do **not** refund credits. Once a key has been delivered and a credit consumed, that credit is spent — even if the customer later requests a refund.
</Info>

Unused credits roll over month to month while your subscription remains active. If you run out of credits mid-period, auto-delivery pauses immediately. Your inventory, integrations, product data, and team access stay completely intact — only new deliveries are paused until you top up or upgrade.

## Finding your billing page

Open **Billing & Plans** from the sidebar. This page is only visible to **Super Admins** and **Admins** — Support and Vendor roles do not have access.

On the Billing & Plans page you'll find:

- A **live usage widget** showing credits consumed and credits remaining in the current period.
- A **transaction summary** with a history of deliveries billed against your plan.
- Your current plan details and the option to upgrade.

Currency formatting on the billing page is mapped dynamically to your store's default currency.

## Upgrading your plan

You can upgrade your plan in two ways:

1. Click the **upgrade banner** at the top of the main dashboard.
2. Navigate to **Billing & Plans → Upgrade** and select your new plan.

Plan changes take effect immediately — your new credit allowance is available the moment you confirm the upgrade.

<Tip>
  If you're consistently using more than 250 credits per month on Starter, upgrading to Growth at $40/month is more cost-effective than staying on Starter and watching auto-delivery pause. Growth's 1,000 credits works out to $0.04 per key delivered.
</Tip>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="How exactly do credits work?">
    One credit equals one key delivered to a buyer. This applies to every delivery method: automatic fulfilment through an integration, a manual order you log yourself, a marketplace order, and replacement keys sent to resolve a ticket. Each delivery consumes exactly one credit, regardless of the product's price or the channel it was sold on. Unused credits roll over while your subscription is active.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when I run out of credits?">
    Auto-delivery pauses as soon as your credit balance reaches zero. Incoming orders are held rather than fulfilled, but no data is lost — your inventory, integrations, products, and team all stay intact. To resume delivery, either upgrade to a higher plan or wait for your credits to renew at the start of your next billing period. You can upgrade instantly from the **Billing & Plans** page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I switch plans or cancel anytime?">
    Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time from your **Billing & Plans** page. There are no lock-in contracts and no cancellation fees. If you downgrade mid-period, you keep the higher credit allowance until the end of the current billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you charge extra per integration?">
    No. Every supported marketplace and sales channel integration — G2A, Driffle, Eneba, Kinguin, Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and all others — is included on every paid plan at no extra cost. You can connect as many channels as you need without affecting your bill.
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
