Tokensupply is the all-in-one operating system for digital goods sellers. Before you can list products, manage inventory, or connect sales channels, you need a store. This guide walks you through every step — from your first sign-up to having a fully configured workspace ready for your team.

## Sign up and create your store

<Steps>

  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [tokensupply.in](https://tokensupply.in) and sign up with your email address. After submitting the registration form, check your inbox for a verification email and click the confirmation link.

    Once your email is verified, Tokensupply takes you directly to the **Create Store** screen.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Name your store and choose your region">
    Fill in three fields to initialise your store:

    - **Store Name** — the public-facing name customers and integrations will see.
    - **Country** — used for tax defaults, address formatting, and compliance fields.
    - **Default Currency** — the store currency shown on invoices, PDFs, and the dashboard KPI cards.

    Click **Create Store** to continue. The account that creates the store is automatically assigned the **Super Admin** role.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure your business details">
    Open **Settings → General** to complete your business profile. These fields appear on customer invoices, purchase orders, and outbound emails, so fill them in accurately before you start selling.

    | Field | Purpose |
    |---|---|
    | **Store Name** | Your public brand displayed to customers |
    | **Company Name** | Legal entity name shown on invoices and POs |
    | **Legal Name** | Full registered business name for compliance |
    | **Support Email** | Reply-to address on all customer-facing emails |
    | **Address** | Business address printed on invoices and PDFs |
    | **GST / PAN** | Tax identification numbers for applicable regions |
    | **Default Currency** | Store-wide currency (changeable here after setup) |
    | **Logo** | Appears in the topbar, customer portal, emails, and PDFs |

    Your branding — logo and brand colours — and the **Order ID Format** are all configured on this same **General** tab, so complete everything in one pass.

    <Tip>
      Upload your logo as a square PNG or SVG with a transparent background for the cleanest result across all surfaces (topbar, emails, and PDFs).
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customise your storefront URL">
    Open **Settings → Store Page** to edit your storefront slug, which Tokensupply calls the `token_id`. Your public store URL is:

    ```
    tokensupply.in/s/{token_id}
    ```

    Choose a slug that matches your brand — for example, `tokensupply.in/s/acmegames`. You can also toggle whether the public store page is visible to anonymous visitors from this same screen.

    <Note>
      If you are on the Growth plan, you can point a custom domain to your store instead of using the default `tokensupply.in/s/{token_id}` URL. Configure that under **Settings → Custom Domain** once your store is live.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite your team">
    Navigate to **Team → Invite Member** and enter your team member's email address. Select the appropriate role for each person before sending the invitation.

    | Role | Best for |
    |---|---|
    | **Super Admin** | Store owner — full access including billing and plan management |
    | **Admin** | Operations leads — manages team, billing, integrations, and settings |
    | **Support** | Day-to-day work — handles tickets, inventory, and customer messages |
    | **Vendor** | External suppliers — limited to their own purchase orders and offers |

    Each role unlocks a tailored sidebar and a scoped permission set, so team members only see what they need.

    <Warning>
      Only Super Admins and Admins can access **Billing & Plans**. Make sure you assign this role to anyone who needs to manage your subscription or credits.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

</Steps>
