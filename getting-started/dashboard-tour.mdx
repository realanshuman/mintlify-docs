The Tokensupply dashboard is the first screen you see after logging in, and it's designed to give you a complete picture of your store's health in a single glance. Five KPI cards surface the metrics that matter most, a quick-action toolbar gets you to common tasks in one click, and a structured sidebar puts every module within easy reach. This tour explains each element so you can navigate with confidence from day one.

## KPI cards

The five cards at the top of the dashboard summarise your store's current state in your store currency. All numeric values use tabular figures so columns stay aligned when you're scanning across cards.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Revenue" icon="circle-dollar-sign">
    Net revenue from completed orders, adjusted for refunds. Reflects only fulfilled transactions — pending or cancelled orders are excluded.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Orders" icon="bag-shopping">
    Total completed transactions processed across all connected sales channels during the selected period.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Active Inventory" icon="boxes-stacked">
    The number of keys currently in **Available** status — ready to be reserved and delivered to a buyer.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Open Tickets" icon="ticket">
    Support tickets that are still awaiting action from your team. Click through to the Tickets module to triage them.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Credits Remaining" icon="bolt">
    Your live billing usage at a glance. Shows how many delivery credits you have left in the current billing period.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Keep an eye on **Credits Remaining** as you approach the end of your billing period. When credits run out, auto-delivery pauses — upgrading from **Billing & Plans** restores it immediately.
</Tip>

## Quick actions

The quick-action toolbar below the KPI cards gives you one-click access to the four most common tasks, so you can act without navigating away from the dashboard:

- **Create product** — jump straight to the new product form.
- **Add inventory** — open the inventory entry panel for a product.
- **Log order** — manually record an order that came in outside a connected channel.
- **Open ticket** — start a new support ticket linked to a customer or order.

## Sidebar navigation

The sidebar is organised into three named sections. Hover the sidebar edge and click to collapse it into an icon-only rail when you need more screen space.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Main">
    The **Main** section contains your core operational modules:

    | Item | What you'll find there |
    |---|---|
    | **Dashboard** | KPI cards, quick actions, and live order feed |
    | **Products** | Your product catalogue with status and pricing |
    | **Purchases** | Purchase Orders and Vendor management |
    | **Sales** | Customers and Invoices |
    | **Tickets** | Customer support issues and resolutions |
    | **Messages** | Team chat and internal notes |
    | **Orders** | All Orders list and the Create Order form |
    | **Analytics** | Charts and trend data for revenue and fulfilment |
    | **Reports** | Exportable tabular reports |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Sales Channels">
    The **Sales Channels** section manages where and how you sell:

    | Item | What you'll find there |
    |---|---|
    | **Integrations** | Connect and configure marketplace and storefront integrations (G2A, Driffle, Eneba, Kinguin, Shopify, WooCommerce, and more) |
    | **Sales Channel** | Manage per-channel settings, pricing rules, and sync status |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Store">
    The **Store** section covers workspace configuration:

    | Item | What you'll find there |
    |---|---|
    | **Team** | Invite members and manage roles |
    | **Settings** | General, Store Page, Custom Domain, and other store-wide configuration |
    | **Templates** | Email and document templates for customer-facing communications |
    | **Billing & Plans** | Subscription, credit usage, and upgrade options (Super Admin and Admin only) |
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Universal search

Press **⌘K** on macOS or **Ctrl+K** on Windows and Linux to open the universal search palette. The 360 px command palette lets you jump to any record in your store without clicking through the sidebar:

- Products and SKUs
- Vendors and purchase orders
- Customers and invoices
- Transactions and orders

Start typing and results appear instantly. Press **Enter** to navigate directly to the matched record.

<Info>
  Universal search is available from every page in the app — you don't need to be on the dashboard to use it. Make it your default way to navigate once you're familiar with the sidebar structure.
</Info>
