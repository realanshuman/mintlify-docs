Tokensupply keeps a central customer directory so every order, invoice, and interaction stays connected to a real contact record. Rather than entering customer details each time you create an order, you build a reusable profile once and reference it wherever you need it — from manual order creation to invoice generation. Each customer gets a unique sequential identifier, making them easy to find and reference across the platform.

## Adding a customer

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Customers list">
    Go to **Sales → Customers** in the sidebar to open the customer directory.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add Customer">
    Select **Add Customer** to open the customer creation form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the customer type">
    Tag the customer as either **Individual** or **Business**. This distinction carries through to invoices and helps you apply the right tax treatment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the details and save">
    Enter the customer's name, email address, and any other relevant details. Save the record — Tokensupply assigns the next available sequential identifier (for example, `CUS-001`, `CUS-002`) automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Customer IDs follow a sequential format — `CUS-001`, `CUS-002`, and so on. IDs are assigned in order and never reused, so you can always reference a customer by their ID in search fields and filters.
</Info>

## Customer detail page

Clicking any customer in the directory opens their detail page. The layout uses a profile header with quick stats at the top and an underline tab bar below it for navigating between different views of the customer's data.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Overview" icon="user">
    Contact details, customer type (Individual or Business), and a summary of activity at a glance.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Orders" icon="bag-shopping">
    Every manual order associated with this customer, with status badges and quick links to each transaction detail page.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Invoices" icon="file-invoice">
    All invoices raised for this customer, including draft and confirmed invoices with their current status and totals.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Activity" icon="clock-rotate-left">
    A chronological log of actions taken on the customer record — creation, edits, order associations, and internal notes.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Internal notes

The detail page also supports internal notes — freeform text visible only to your team. Use notes to record account context, special pricing arrangements, or any other information that helps your team serve the customer consistently.

<Tip>
  Internal notes are never visible to the customer. They appear only on the customer detail page inside your Tokensupply workspace.
</Tip>

## Using customers in orders and invoices

When you create a manual order or an invoice, Tokensupply provides an inline customer picker to associate a customer with the record. You have two options:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Search for existing customer">
    Start typing in the customer field to search your directory by:

    - **Name** — full or partial match
    - **Email address** — useful when customers share a name
    - **CUS-ID** — the fastest lookup if you have the identifier to hand (for example, `CUS-042`)

    Select the matching result to link the customer to the order or invoice.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Create customer inline">
    If the customer doesn't exist in your directory yet, click **Create customer** directly inside the customer picker — no need to navigate away from the order or invoice form. The new customer record is saved to your directory and linked to the current record simultaneously.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
