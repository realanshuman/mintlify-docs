## How Tokensupply works

Every digital goods workflow flows through four stages:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Catalog your products">
    Add products with SKUs, categories, and delivery settings. Configure once and reuse across every channel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Source inventory from vendors">
    Compare vendor offers side-by-side, raise purchase orders, and upload keys directly into the encrypted vault.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your sales channels">
    Link Shopify, G2A, Eneba, Kinguin, and other marketplaces. Map products and enable auto-delivery.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deliver and support">
    Orders are fulfilled automatically. Customers reveal keys through a branded portal. Issues are handled through the built-in ticketing system.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key capabilities

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Encrypted Key Vault" icon="lock">
    AES-256 encrypted storage. Keys are masked by default — every reveal is logged with a full audit trail.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multi-Channel Selling" icon="arrows-split-up-and-left">
    One product, every channel. Manage listings across Shopify, G2A, Eneba, and more from a single view.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Vendor Management" icon="handshake">
    Manage suppliers, compare offers, raise purchase orders, and track spend — all in one place.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Customer Delivery Portal" icon="envelope-open-text">
    Branded, tokenised delivery links. Customers reveal keys at their convenience; old links auto-invalidate on refund.
  </Card>
  <Card title="REST API & Webhooks" icon="webhook">
    Programmatic access to orders, inventory, products, and more. Real-time events via signed webhooks.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Credit-Based Billing" icon="coins">
    Pay only for what you deliver. 1 key delivered = 1 credit. No seat fees, no per-feature gates.
  </Card>
